How to Use

  1. First, download a cookie extractor extension. You can use:
    • CookieMaster
    • Chrome Webstore Link
  2. Go to the cookies page and click on the "Copy" button.
  3. After copying, click on the extension you installed and select "Import Cookies".
  4. Paste the copied cookies into the extension's provided space.
Note: Ensure you have udemy.com opened in a tab before pasting the cookies into it.
